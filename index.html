ARTIST PROFILE

D.Laughing

Hip-Hop & R&B Artist / Storyteller

Officially signing with ae Label on April 24, 2026, D.Laughing is a rising voice whose musical style beautifully fuses Hip-Hop, R&B, and emotional, soul-stirring storytelling.

Steering away from conventional commercial templates, his music explores deeply personal feelings, introspective thoughts, and a distinct perspective on love, growth, and lived experiences. His ultimate strength lies in narrative power, crafting immersive atmospheric records where listeners can easily find pieces of their own journeys.

Our Story

ae Label is a music management and distribution company, built with the mission of accompanying the young generation of artists on their journey to develop a professional music career.

We believe that every artist carries a unique color – and ae Label's duty is to help those colors shine in the right way, right direction, and at the right time.

More than just a management unit, ae Label is also a bridge connecting artists – music – audiences, through creative projects, release strategies, and long-term image orientation.

What we do?

  • ⭐ Management & development of young artists
  • ⭐ Music distribution across all streaming platforms
  • ⭐ Visual & stylistic identity orientation
  • ⭐ Concept curation & release strategy planning
  • ⭐ Connecting artists with audiences through creative projects

Mission

Accompanying Vietnamese artists in building a sustainable, professional, and unique musical career, while bringing music closer to both domestic and international audiences.

Vision

To become a youthful, creative, and professional music ecosystem where artists can freely develop their individuality and reach further in the international market.
